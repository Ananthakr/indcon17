Benefits

By being a sponsor for Indcon, an organization would have the following tangible benefits :
1. Sponsor's logo on all official documents and publicity posters
2. Sponsor's name prominent in all media ads and t-shirts
3. Video advertisement during the event
4. Sponsor's ads on the official souvenir distributed to the guests as well as supporting organizations of the event
5. Ability to distribute brochures/flyers


Main Sponser

1.The event will be named after the main sponsor, i.e. Society of Industrial Engineers & Company name present
2. The sponsor's name and logo will be printed on the t-shirts and delegate kits
3. The name, logo and a link to the website of the company will be prominently mentioned in the Indcon website
4. All publications will bear the name and logo of main sponsor 5. Stalls will be provided at the college premises

Co-Sponser

1. All publications leading to the event will bear the name and logo of the company
2. Logo and link to the website of the company will be displayed in the website
3. Stalls will be provided at the college premises

 

Event Sponser

  1. The name of the event will be marketed along with the name of the event sponsor
2. All publications will bear the name and logo of the company
3. The name and logo of the company will be displayed at the site of the event and in the indcon website
4. Sponsor's name and its logo will be published in souvenir 

Delegate Kit & T- Shirt Sponser

1. The name and logo of the company will be placed in T-shirt, Delegate kit and Indcon website prominently
2. Sponsor's name and logo will be printed in the souvenir
